Notting Hill Carnival

The Notting Hill Carnival is an annual event that since 1966 has taken place on the streets of Notting Hill, London, UK each August, over three days. It is led by members of the West Indian community. The carnival has attracted around one million people in the past years, making it one of the largest street festivals in the world.
